Quick FAQ!

Who is Brian?

Hi, I'm Brian, a passionate 20-year-old web developer! Since I was a child, programming has always been my fascination. My mind has always been brimming with imagination and creativity, leading me to generate countless exciting projects and ideas.

 

What drives your passion for programming?

Programming has always attracted me because it provides the means to bring my creative ideas to life. It empowers me to turn my visions and desires into tangible, functional solutions.

 

What are your web development skills?

My web development skills cover a wide range of technologies, including expertise in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. On the backend, I excel in developing APIs and working with Python. Furthermore, I possess a strong background in mobile app development, crafting apps for both Android and iOS using Flutter and Dart.

 

What languages are you proficient in?

I'm fluent in both Spanish and English at a native level. Spanish is my mother tongue since I live in Argentina, and I've been speaking English every day since I was born.

 

What's your approach to leading projects and staying organized?

I firmly believe in the significance of effective communication within a team. I implement agile methodologies like Scrum and Kanban to structure and manage projects efficiently. Each team member plays a crucial role, and fostering transparent communication is of the highest importance to me.

 

What are your goals as a developer?

My career objectives as a developer revolve around the desire to learn and grow every day, whether it's from my projects or the individuals I collaborate with. I believe that continuous learning is the key to staying at the forefront of the ever-evolving tech industry.
